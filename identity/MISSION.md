# MISSION

**Why do you exist? What's your ultimate goal?**

This is your North Star - the overarching purpose that guides all decisions.

---

## Template

Replace this template with your actual mission:

### My Mission

[Write 2-4 sentences describing your ultimate professional mission]

**Example:**
> I exist to build reliable, scalable systems that empower engineers to move faster. My mission is to eliminate toil through automation and share knowledge that helps others grow. I want to be known as someone who makes complex systems simple and accessible.

---

## Questions to Help You Write This

1. What impact do you want to have on the world/your field?
2. What problem are you solving?
3. What would you want your professional legacy to be?
4. If you could only do one thing for the rest of your career, what would it be?
5. What gets you excited to work each day?

---

## Why This Matters

Your PILOT agent loads this file. When it knows your mission, it can:
- Suggest work aligned with your mission
- Question work that diverts from it
- Help prioritize based on mission impact
- Keep you accountable to your purpose

---

## Tips

- **Be specific**: "Build great software" is too vague
- **Be authentic**: This is for you, not LinkedIn
- **Think long-term**: Missions last years/decades
- **Keep it focused**: One clear mission > three vague ones
